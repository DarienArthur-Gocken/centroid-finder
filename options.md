Option 1: JavaCV
Pros: 
    - Best codec/format coverage
    - Good for both video I/O and image processing 

Cons:
    - Native libraries
    - More complex


Option 2: JCodec
Pros: 
    - Pure java, no native libraries
    - Easy to manage

Cons:
    - Less codec support
    - Slower for complex videos


Option 3: Video4j
Pros: 
    - Simple
    - Self contained

Cons:
    - Less codec support
    - Less actively maintained and smaller than the others
